## Previous Experiences

A couple of summers ago I interned at [Fractal AI Research](https://fractal.ai/ai-research), where I worked on Diffusion and Reward Models.

Before that I had been engaged as a Student Researcher in the intersection of AI and HCI at the [MIT Media Lab](https://www.media.mit.edu/) in Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I worked on creating an [agentic memory-layer](https://www.media.mit.edu/projects/memoro/overview/) to help aged people recollect memories.

I also worked in an early stage deep-tech startup, [Dirac Labs](https://diraclabs.com) for almost an entire year, wearing as many hats as I needed to.

I am also an [Emergent Ventures](https://marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2025/11/emergent-ventures-india-13th-cohort.html) grantee. I won the grant initially to go to MIT as a full-time student researcher, but I ended up using it to build an AI tool to aid structural engineers with grunt work.
